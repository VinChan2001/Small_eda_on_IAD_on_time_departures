Methodology & Data Quality

Dataset Characteristics

  • 92,650 departure records
  • July 2017 - December 2024
  • 61 unique destinations
  • 5 major carriers (UA, DL, WN, AA, AS)

  • Temporal Analysis: Year, month, day-of-week patterns
